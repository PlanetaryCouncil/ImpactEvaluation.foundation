Envisioning a new approach towards measuring impact, externalities, cobenefits.

We need to account for the full cost of the products, goods, services. Meat is too cheap. Energy is too cheap. The real cost is outsourced to nature, vulnerable communities, future generations. Currently free market is captured by subsidies and regulation, loads of stuff is artificially too cheap as it doesn't take into account the full cost that is externalised elsewhere.

Our first initiative is to build an educational programme for impact evaluators "on the ground". Not flying fancy experts to measure CO2 and eco / bio / regen / organic / fair trade practices, but local person $10 per hour with mobile phone and internet, much faster, much cheaper, much more scalable. Tapping into Web3 technologies: attestations, proof of location, reputation, dispute resolution, token engineering, game theory. We are determined to win, we are the change that needs to happen in the world.
